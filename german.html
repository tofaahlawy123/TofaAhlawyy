Player Profile: Manuel Neuer was born in Gelsenkirchen in Germany on the 27th of March 1986. His talent and passion for football appeared since he was young. He played his first match with Schalke FC juniors at the age of five and continued at the club until he signed his first professional contract with him in 2005. He participated for the first time in A match in the German league season 2006-2007 with Schalke. He moved to his current club, Bayern Munich, in 2011.
Neuer won many titles such as the German Bundesliga, the German Cup, the European Champions League and the 2014 World Cup with his country, and was the best goalkeeper in that tournament. He was named the best German player in 2011 and 2014, and the best goalkeeper in the world in 2013, 2014, and 2015
