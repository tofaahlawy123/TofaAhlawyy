Zinedine Zidane

Player Profile: Zinedine Zidane was born in a suburb of Marseille in France on June 23, 1972 to Algerian parents. Then he moved to Bordeaux and then Juventus before ending his career in Real Madrid and then becoming their coach recently. Zidane won league titles in Italy and Spain, the Champions League as player and coach, the World Cup and the European Nations Cup with the French national team.

Thierry Henry

Player Profile: Thierry Daniel Henry is a retired French footballer. He started his professional football career in the youth ranks in Monaco under the guidance of coach Assen Wenger and from there he moved to Juventus, where he played mainly as a left winger. Henry transformed into a striker after 2000, becoming one of the most feared players in Europe. He moved to Arsenal and then to Barcelona where the team captured the Champions League trophy in the 2008-2009 season. His style of play was distinguished by being ostentatious, fruitful and complete, and he is the only player in European football history to have been named Footballer of the Year for three years as He was named French Player of the Year on four different occasions. Henry is known as the ninth most sought-after player in the world due to his huge popularity among the public.

Paul Pogba

Player Profile: Paul Pogba is a French footballer who plays for the French national team and for the English club Manchester United. In 2014, he became the most expensive player in the world. He started his professional career with Manchester United in 2011, then moved the following year to join Italian club Juventus. Thanks to his outstanding performance, his team achieved a string of victories while winning several world titles. In 2013, he was awarded the Golden Boy award, and led his French national football team to the FIFA Junior World Cup title. He also returned home with the 'Best Player' award at the end of the tournament in 2013, and was named the Young Player of the Year at the 2014 World Cup.

N'Golo Kante

Player Profile: N'Golo Kante played in Boulogne, then spent two seasons at Caen in the First Division. In 2015, he joined Leicester City for a reported sum of £5.6m and became a regular starter after winning the Premier League. The following year, he joined Chelsea for £32m, winning the league again in his first season. He also won the PFA Player of the Year that year and became the first player on the pitch to win back-to-back Premier League titles with different clubs after Eric Cantona in 1992 and 1993. Kante made his French debut in 2016, and he was included in the squad that finished runner-up at that year's European Championship. Two years later, he was a key player in the French national team that won the 2018 FIFA World Cup.

Griezmann

Player Profile: Griezmann was born and raised in the French city of Macon, and was an ambitious player since his early years, but he suffered from genetic diseases that did not allow him to grow in height or shape, and therefore was rejected by several youth clubs. 2005 was a year full of hope for Griezmann, as he was noticed by several clubs including Spanish professional club Real Sociedad at the same time he was playing for local club Montpellier. In the 2009-2010 season, he made his debut with Sociedad, where he proved his prowess in the previous season, scoring five goals in four matches in which he participated. Griezmann had four successful matches after that season with Sociedad, after which he made his way to Atletico Madrid in July 2014.Griezmann's good play led to his signing with Atletico Madrid in 2016, which also guarantees him to stay at the club until 2021. As for the main national team, Griezmann played in the 2014 World Cup and the European League in 2016, in which he won the Golden Boot award for his successful passes that led to several goals.
